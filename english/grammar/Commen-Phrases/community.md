# 社区短语

## 一

*   Is there a bank close to here?

    这附近有银行吗？

*   Yes, there is a bank near the park?
    是的，在公园的附近有银行。

*   What about a supermaket?
    超市呢？

*   There are two supermarkets on this street.
    街上有两家超市。
